Biography

I am an editor, language teacher and writer based in Kenya and France. I have always been inspired by language and culture and most of my professional and personal work revolves around having to learn more and share my knowledge with others. Most of my previous projects have been based on three languages: English, French and Swahili.

Qualifications and certifications

I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Education (French, Swahili) with Information Technology from Maseno University in Kenya.

I am a certified teacher with the Kenya Teachers’ Service Commission and a foreign language assistant.

I hold an advanced certificate for the Diplôme d’etudes en langue Française

Personal story

When I was young, I was a really curious girl. I always wanted to know how the world works. I would always borrow big literature books with difficult words than my mind could fathom.I would spend countless hours stuck in stories about other places, lands I believed I could get to discover someday. I didn’t know that this was some sort of escapism back then nut it was intriguing, learning so much about the world, about different people. That was the beginning of my passion; of course I didn’t know this then. Now when I help people tell there stories through editing, when I help them towards their journey of sharing who they are and what they represent, when they feel heard, I feel complete because another person elsewhere in the world will share in that joy that comes from diversity or people relating to the stories will feel included and he world will always be a better place with good stories.
